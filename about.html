About me

I am a Computer Technologies Engineering (ITC) student at Tecnológico de Monterrey, with a strong interest in web development and the creation of visual and interactive experiences.

I enjoy combining technology, creativity, and design to build functional and engaging digital solutions. My work focuses on clean interfaces, intuitive user experiences, and meaningful projects.

Beyond technology, I am passionate about dance and sports, which have helped me develop discipline, teamwork, and creativity — skills that I bring into my professional projects.
